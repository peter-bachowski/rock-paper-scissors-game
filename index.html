Rock Paper Scissors Game

Welcome!

This game is meant to showcase my javascript abilities that I have learned thus far in my journey through The Odin Project. Hope you enjoy!



You will be playing against a computer opponent in this Rock, Paper, Scissors game. The game will consist of three rounds, where the winner of two out of the three rounds will be the winner. You must choose one of three symbols, where each one beats another, and vice versa can lose to another.
You must choose rock, paper or scissors by clicking the respective button. Good luck!
